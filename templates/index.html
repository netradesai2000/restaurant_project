ABOUT US

For the love of delicious food

Welcome to our culinary fusion at Sanskruti! Indulge in a harmonious blend of Maharashtrian, Chinese, and Punjabi cuisines under one roof. Our menu invites you to savor the authentic spices of Maharashtra, the savory delights of Chinese wok-tossed creations, and the rich flavors of Punjabi classics. Each dish is a celebration of diverse tastes, meticulously prepared to tantalize your palate. Join us for a gastronomic journey where Maharashtrian, Chinese, and Punjabi traditions unite to create a memorable dining experience.
